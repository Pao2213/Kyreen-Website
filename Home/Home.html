Welcome to MOWCAG Church located in Tanay, Rizal! We are a community of believers committed to sharing God’s love and helping people grow in faith. Through passionate worship, biblical teaching, and outreach, we create a welcoming environment where you can experience God’s transformative power, overcome life’s challenges, and fulfill His purpose. Whether you're seeking faith, healing, or a place to belong, MOWCAG is here to help you connect with Christ and live a victorious life.
